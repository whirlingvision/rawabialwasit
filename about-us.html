About Sama AlBurj

Sama AlBurj is an ISO Certified Trading and Contracting Company" located at Al Khobar, KSA. Our company is principally involved in Trading, Engineering and Fabrication. We are a prominent and leading KSA owned company operating Nationally and Internationally. Our company takes a pride in its ability to provide an unparalleled service to the customers and partners.

Sama AlBurj is Specialized in Mechanical, Safety, Electrical, Stainless Steel, Steel, MS-Sheets, Valves, Furniture and Fittings. We are an Approved Vendor for most of the leading companies and contractors.

We are Experts in providing solutions for the workers who are working at heights.
